Man, 26, leaps to death in front of train in Union Square station

Author: NEW YORK DAILY NEWS Date: Wednesday, March 2, 2016, 7:33 AM

NYPD ESU Detectives work to remove a person who was struck and killed by the uptown 6 train early Wednesday morning at Union Square station in Manhattan.

A 26-year-old man leaped in front of an oncoming train and to his death inside a Union Square train station in an apparent suicide early Wednesday, police said. The victim, who was not named jumped in front of a uptown number 6 train on E. 14th St. near Broadway around 12:20 a.m., police said. The man died at the scene, police said. Cops were investigating the scene Wednesday. The victim's name will not be released pending family notification.
